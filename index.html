Hi, I am Yang Zhao, a Professor and PhD advisor of Computer Science and Technology at the International Research Institute for Artificial Intelligence, Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. My research interests and expertise span sensing, signal processsing, edge computing, and artificial intelligence systems.

I was a Lead Research Engineer at GE Global Research, an Adjunct Professor at University at Albany, and a Research Intern at Microsoft Research. I receive my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Utah, and I am an IEEE senior member.

I have worked in various research domains including atmospheric remote sensing, industrial inspection, smart facilities, precision medical imaging, etc. I am always open to new ideas and research opportunities. If you would like to work together, feel free to contact me.
