Hi, I am Yang Zhao, a research professor of the International Research Institute for Artificial Intelligence at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. My research interests and expertise span sensing, signal processsing, mobile computing, embedded and intelligent systems.

I was a Lead Research Engineer at GE Global Research, an Adjunct Professor at University at Albany, and a Research Intern at Microsoft Research. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Utah under Professor Neal Patwari.

I have worked in various research domains including atmospheric remote sensing, industrial inspection, smart facilities, precision medical imaging, etc. I am always looking for people to explore new ideas and research opportunities. If you would like to work together, feel free to contact me.
