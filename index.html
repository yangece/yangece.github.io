Hi, I am Yang Zhao, a Full Research Professor and PhD advisor at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen. My research interests and expertise span sensing, edge computing, and artificial intelligence systems.

I was a Lead Research Engineer at GE Global Research, an Adjunct Professor at University at Albany, and a Research Intern at Microsoft Research. I receive my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Utah, and I am an IEEE senior member.

I have worked in various research domains including atmospheric remote sensing, industrial inspection, smart facilities, precision medical imaging, etc. I am always open to new ideas and research opportunities. If you would like to work together, feel free to contact me.

  • Underground Potato Root Tuber Sensing via a Wireless Network , our wireless sensing demo won the Best Demo Runner-up at the IPSN Conference in May 2024.
  • DVFO: Learning-Based DVFS for Energy-Efficient Edge-Cloud Collaborative Inference, our paper was accepted by IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing in January 2024.
  • Octopus: SLO-Aware Progressive Inference Serving via Deep Reinforcement Learning in Multi-Tenant Edge Cluster, PhD student Ziyang's paper was presented at the 21st International Conference on Service Oriented Computing (ICSOC) in Rome, Italy in November 2023 (acceptance rate 17%).
